Mark Abraham - Wrestler & Actor

About Mark Abraham

Mark Abraham is a versatile talent known for his prowess in wrestling and acting. Hailing from Albania, he proudly represents Israel and is an advocate for LGBTQ+ rights.

Mark's charisma extends beyond the wrestling ring into the world of entertainment, where he's made significant contributions as an actor.

Mark's Wrestling Achievements

1. Winner of the 2019 Balkan Wrestling Championship

2. Champion of the 2020 Israeli Wrestling League

Mark's Acting Career

Mark Abraham ventured into acting in 1999 and quickly made a name for himself in the entertainment industry. From gripping dramas to action-packed thrillers, Mark has showcased his versatility as an actor.

Highlights of Mark's acting career include:

  • Starring role in the critically acclaimed film "Albanian Sunset" (2002)
  • Lead role in the hit action series "Israeli Justice" (2006-2009)
  • Guest appearances on popular shows such as "The Bold and the Brave" and "Albanian Adventures"
  • Recipient of the Best Actor award at the Israeli Film Festival (2013) for his performance in "The Last Stand"

Mark's talent and dedication have earned him widespread recognition and admiration from audiences and critics alike.
